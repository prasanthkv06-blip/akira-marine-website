## 1. Project Overview

### 1.1 Company Background
Akira Marine Services Private Limited specializes in the maintenance of Wärtsilä dual-fuel four-stroke engines, LNG-related ship services, and onboard workshop operations. Additional services include hull cleaning, painting, and general marine engine maintenance.

### 1.2 Vision Statement
> "To be the global standard of excellence in specialized LNG and marine engineering services, recognized for our unwavering commitment to safety, precision, and the seamless integration of modern innovation with classic maritime craftsmanship."

### 1.3 Mission Statement
> "Our mission is to ensure the operational integrity of our clients' vessels by delivering superior on-site workshop services, expert engine maintenance, and comprehensive hull care. We are dedicated to protecting our clients' assets and the environment through meticulous work, continuous crew training, and an unyielding focus on safety."

### 1.4 Core Focus
The website must prominently feature the company’s specialization in **field service maintenance for Wärtsilä dual-fuel 4-stroke engines**. This is the key differentiator and should be communicated clearly to establish authority and attract the right clients.

### 1.5 Project Goal
Build a modern, classic, high-performance website that:
- Showcases technical expertise in LNG and dual-fuel engine maintenance.
- Builds trust with ship owners and operators through authentic content and professional design.
- Generates client inquiries via clear calls-to-action and contact forms.
- Reflects the company’s commitment to safety, precision, and innovation.
- **Achieves top search engine rankings** for relevant keywords to maximize visibility.
- **Is secure by design**, protecting against cyber attacks, cloning, leeching, and data breaches.
